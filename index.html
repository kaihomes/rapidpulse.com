RapidPulse

RapidPulse provides professional, cost effective medical and safety services for film and television productions, extreme events, and government agencies. Our integrated view on medical and safety requirements, experience in remote wilderness areas, background in adventure sports, and ability to look at a production with a business mind sets us apart. We operate globally.

Our services cover all the:

  • operations and logistics - preparation, implementation and post production or event management
  • appropriate staffing levels - We reduce the staff required by integrating knowledgeable staff that can handle both the safety, wilderness and medical requirements.
  • medical supplies and safety equipment.

Who We Are

Our lead doctor, Matt Conklin, is currently an Emergency Room Physician with Idaho Emergency Physicians, which manages the emergency departments for all Saint Alphonsus hospitals in the Boise Metro area. He is also associate medical director for Life Flight Network and previously directed Ada County Paramedics.

Operations are led by Mark Joffe. Mark has been in the adventure industry since 1991 and worked globally on rivers and for adventure companies. Some of the highlights include working as manager for Shearwater Adventures rafting operations on the Zambezi river 1995-1997. He has also guided and managed companies in Australia, Morocco, and Ecuador, as well as throughout the United States. He has managed events and filming in Morocco, Zimbabwe, Zambia, Costa Rica, Chile, Ecuador and South Africa. Mark is currently based in Boise, Idaho.
